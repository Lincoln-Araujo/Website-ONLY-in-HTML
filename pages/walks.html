Walks

* return to Hobbies

One thing I love to do after a entire day of work is to go for a nice walk outside.

But, some days a I'm so full of energy and I do walks a little more audacious.

Let me tell you about them:

  • Walking in the city

    Walking between buildings and houses is somehting that I do constantly.

    It's a perfect way to relax at the end of the day and organize all my thoughts after a long and hard day.

    It is also the best way to get to know new places while traveling.

  • Walking in the woods

    Sometimes, hiking in the woods is the best way to recharge and feel better, especially when I'm feeling down.

    This is a chance for appreciating some nature landscapes and also to get some fresh air.

    However, it can be a little dangerous depending on the daytime.

  • Walking in the mountains

    I can't say this is the best kind of walk (IT IS!), but I always have the best experiences when I do them.

    I love the feeling of seeing everything from the top of a mountain.
